About Service Assessment Ready

Service Assessment Ready is a free learning platform built for civil servants, contractors and delivery teams preparing for GOV.UK service assessments across UK government.

All content is based on published GOV.UK service assessment reports and the official Service Manual guidance. The failure patterns, evidence checklists, and readiness questions reflect real assessment outcomes — not theory.

The platform covers the 14 Service Standard points, the assessment process at Alpha, Beta and Live phases, GDD roles, P3M frameworks (PRINCE2, MSP, MoP), WCAG 2.2 accessibility compliance, and GovS 002 governance.

This platform itself is built to WCAG 2.2 AA compliance — skip links, focus management, keyboard navigation, screen reader support, reduced motion, and minimum touch targets throughout.
